## Structured-Prompt-Driven Development methodology and REASONS Canvas

The methodology that SpecArk packages — **Structured-Prompt-Driven
Development (SPDD)** and the **REASONS Canvas** — was developed by
**Wei Zhang** and **Jessie Jie Xia** (Thoughtworks) and published as
["Structured-Prompt-Driven Development"](https://martinfowler.com/articles/structured-prompt-driven/)
on martinfowler.com (28 April 2026).

The article text on martinfowler.com is © its authors and is not redistributed
in this repository. Concept names and the workflow itself are credited to the
authors throughout SpecArk's documentation. The reference implementation of
the phase commands is `gszhangwei/open-spdd`, listed above.
